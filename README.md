<h2 align="left">E-Bike Rental Exploratory Data Analysis</h2>

###

<h4 align="left">Executive Summary</h4>

###

<p align="left">Using SQL, I pulled E-bike rental dataset to perform an Exploratory Data Analysis to get an overview of the business performance and later used word to compile an Executive Insight Report to summarise my findings.</p>

###

<h4 align="left">Business Problem</h4>

###

<p align="left">UrbanRoll is an E-bike rental company operating across 25 stations. The executive team want to understand the overall performance of the business and find ways to improve their daily operations.</p>

###

<h4 align="left">Methodology</h4>

###

<p align="left">1. SQL to extract the data and performed EDA and visualized the results.<br>2. Used word to compile a report</p>

###

<h4 align="left">Skills</h4>

###

<p align="left">1. SQL: CTEs, Joins, Case statements, aggregate functions and Data Visualization<br>2. Word: Report writing</p>

###

<h4 align="left">Results & Recommendation</h4>

###

<p align="left">I compiled a report summarising the business performance, the key takeaways and recommendations from the overall analysis include:<br><br>-  The peak demand is at 7:00 AM and 3:00 PM, the users use the bikes to commute to and from work.<br>- The casual customer shows lower volume with higher bike time usage which can be improved by launching weekend explorer pass to increase their frequencies during off-peak hours. <br>- The station with high starting point run out of bikes, implements a discount if the user ends the ride at a source station.<br>- The month-over-month analysis shows a steady growth rate.</p>
